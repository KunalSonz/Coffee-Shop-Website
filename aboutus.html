Our Story

We Began Our Journey In June 2018 With The Launch Of Are First Store In The Vicinity Of Kharghar , Nerul, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. Since Then We Are Growing Exponentially Year On Year By Launching Multiple Cloud Kitchens Around Navi Mumbai.

My Coffee Cup Was Conceived When The Founders Found Limited Space To Hangout And Specially That Served Quality & Tasty Food That Was Value For Money.

Cafes Usually Have A Small Limited Menu That’s Over Priced Or Don’t Provide That Quality That’s Expected, That’s Why Café Sacro Servers One Of The Biggest Menu Around Not Just In Food But Also In Beverages, We Like Are Customers To Have A Wide Variety Of Options To Choose From.

We Are Focused On Giving Are Customers The Best Quality & Value For Their Money So We Use Top Notch Quality Ingredients Sourced From Top Brands Around India, Café Sacro Has Always Stood Strong To Its Value For Money Proposition, We Try Are Best To Provide A Product That’s Value & A Flavourful Experience.

We Strive To Be The First Choice For Our Customers And Partners In Their Journey By Giving Them An Food Experience That’s Designed With Passion & Love Bye Our People, While Delivering Value For Money.
